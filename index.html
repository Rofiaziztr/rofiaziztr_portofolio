Dianaalicollection

Production

Jan - Feb · Laravel · Live

A specialized Smart POS & Inventory system for textile wholesalers. It solves the complexity of tracking large units (Rolls, Cartons, Bales) with varying internal volumes.

  • Dynamic Unit Metadata: Validates inventory per primary unit while storing unique volume details for each item.
  • Category Logic: Different handling for Fabrics (Rolls), Smokrings (Cartons), and Lace (Bales).
  • Stock Splitting: Handles remnants and partial units as new entities with precise volume tracking.
Laravel 11 MySQL Tailwind
